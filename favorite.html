A Sloth Life

There are two main kinds of sloth, the two-toed (belonging to the genus Bradypus) and the three-toed (genus Choloepus)

Characteristics of the two-toed sloth:

  • The Hoffman's two-toed sloth is one of the world's slowest mammals
  • Super sedentary that algae grows on its furry coat
  • The plant gives it a greenish tint that is useful camouflage in the trees

Characteristic of the three-toed sloth

  • The three-toed sloths are considerably more slothful
  • They are built for life in the treetops
  • They spend nearly all of their time aloft, hanging from branches with a powerful grip aided by their long claws
